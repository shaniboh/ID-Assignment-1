History

Debut Date : 08 November 2019

In the first series, the album included the K-pop title song "Wait A Minute" and the love song "For You", which made its official debut in July 2018.

Participated in the Hunan Satellite TV variety show "Happy Camp". This is the group's first variety show.

On 18 July 2018,they released the second album of the group's first music album "THE FIRST". At the same time, they also participated in the opening ceremony of the 4th Jackie Chan International Action Film Week as guest performers.

On 17 August 2018, the third album of the group’s first music album "THE FIRST" was released, which included hip-hop songs "Attention" and "On&On".

On 19 August 2018, it won the Most Popular Group Award at the Asian New Songs Annual Festival; In addition, they also participated in the Mango TV real science fiction experimental program "Brave World".

On 04 September 2018, sang the theme song "Together" for the movie "Gu Jian Qi Tan Zhi Liu Yue Zhao Ming".

On 14 September 2018 participated in Youku Netcom "The Truth!" "Flowers and All Things"; November 3, won the best national style newcomer group award at the Guofeng Bliss Night Music Festival, and sang the song "Together" live.

On 29 November 2018, won the Asian Music Festival Annual Most Influential Men's Group Award.

On 25 March 2019, they won the Most Popular Idol Men’s Group Award and the Asian Popular Men’s Group Award at the 26th Oriental Music Awards; Ma Sichao sang the song "Rocket Family" together.

On 11 May 2019, they won the Asia's Most Popular Pioneer Group Award at the 31st Years of Music Pioneer Chart.

On 31 December 2019, participated in the "Zhejiang Satellite TV 2020 New Year's Eve Party".
